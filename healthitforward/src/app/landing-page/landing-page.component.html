About Us
HealthItForward was inspired by a patient who survived her illness despite her unfavorable medical prognosis. While such patients are considered medical outliers, this forum aims to collect data from these and all patients in order to better understand, treat and ultimately discover cures through this aggregated data. In this manner, the main goal of HealthItForward is to connect patients, caregivers, researchers, and other experts through a network where they can safely share information on both alternative and allopathic medical treatments.
